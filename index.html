The Problem We Solve

Kerala Weddings Are Beautiful.
Planning Them Is Not.

Every family plans meticulously — except for the one thing that determines catering, seating, and budget: how many people are actually coming.

~₹2L

Average food wasted at a Kerala wedding due to inaccurate headcounts

500+

Average guests at a Muslim wedding in Malappuram or Kozhikode — all invited without confirmation

100%

Of NRI families receive physical cards too late or not at all

  • 📋
    Physical cards are distributed but there is no way to know who received them, who read them, or who is actually attending.
  • 📱
    Confirmation happens over fragmented WhatsApp messages and phone calls — none of it consolidated in one place.
  • ✈️
    NRI family members across the Gulf, UK, USA and Canada are left out of the invitation loop until someone calls them individually.
  • 💸
    Caterers and venue managers are given rough estimates that are almost always wrong — leading to excess spend or shortage.
  • 🌐
    No digital-native solution exists for the Kerala market. Global tools don't support Malayalam, don't understand the culture, and aren't built around WhatsApp.
